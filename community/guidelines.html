Community Guidelines — How We Play 🎱

A few ground rules to keep our community respectful, helpful, and fun.

🤝

Be Respectful

Treat every member like a practice partner. Disagree with ideas, not people.

1
🎯

Stay on Topic

Post in the right category. Off-topic goes in the Player Lounge.

2
📹

Credit Sources

Sharing someone else's content? Give credit. Link the original creator or source.

3
🚫

No Spam

Genuine contributions are welcome. Pure ads and self-promotion are not.

4
👶

Help Beginners

Everyone was new once. Answer with patience, or simply scroll past.

5
🔞

Keep It Clean

No NSFW content, excessive profanity, or gambling promotion.

6
🛡️

Report, Don't Retaliate

Use the report button. Let moderators handle it — don't escalate.

7
🏆

Earn Your Reputation

Badges and likes reward quality contributions. There are no shortcuts.

8
⚖️

Mod Decisions Are Final

Disagree with a decision? DM a moderator. No public arguments about moderation.

9

By participating in the 9BallShop community, you agree to follow these guidelines.
